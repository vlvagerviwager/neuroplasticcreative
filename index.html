about

neuroplastic creative: software engineer / analog photographer / artist

I am a full-time software engineer based in Dublin, Ireland. I am looking for opportunities to collaborate and showcase my work as an analog photographer and artist.

I paint with acrylic on canvas. I shoot primarily on film using my Yashica FX-D, Holga 135BC, and Diana Mini.

Site design is in progress and not final. Thanks for your patience!
