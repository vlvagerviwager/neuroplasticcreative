    • Wien in Lomo, self-published

      • Wien in Lomo is inspired by the photographer's first visit to the beautiful city of Vienna, Austria. Armed with a Holga 135BC, acquiring a Diana Mini on the way, and constantly running out of film, Wien in Lomo is an instrument with which to share a wonder and love for Vienna with the rest of the world.
