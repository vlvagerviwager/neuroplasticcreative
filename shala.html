my online yoga shala


I wrote this page because people who know me well know that I am passionate about yoga and love to recommend my favourite teachers and studios to beginners. Instead of emailing people over and over again, I decided to write everything down here.

My very first yoga class was a restorative class in February 2013. I started doing yoga regularly in September 2013 out of curiosity. It unexpectedly healed a painful lower back injury which I never had diagnosed or treated, after only three weeks. I have tried many styles, including restorative, vinyasa, hatha, kundalini, Bikram, hot yoga, Iyengar, and ashtanga. I have been focusing primarily on ashtanga vinyasa yoga since January 2014.

Some goals:

  • I am an RYT 200 yoga teacher trainee, scheduled to complete in July 2016.
  • I will be attending a workshop by one of my inspirations, Kino MacGregor, in Stockholm in 2016!
  • I hope to attend a 10-day Vipassana course sometime in the future.

Ask me more, about my practice, about my experience with any style, any studio, any teacher. Let's talk. (Or contact me directly if you know me.)

my Ashtanga teachers
